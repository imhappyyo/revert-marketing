# Revert — Content Calendar & Cadence

The engine generates daily; this is the **publishing rhythm** to aim for. Volume is set
per channel in `brand.json → channels[*].cadence_per_week`.

## Weekly cadence

| Channel | Posts/wk | Best times (local) | Notes |
|---------|----------|--------------------|-------|
| TikTok | 5 | 7–9am, 12pm, 7–10pm | Hook in 1.5s. Reuse the same cut on Reels + Shorts. |
| Instagram | 5 | 11am, 7–9pm | Reels + 1 before/after carousel/wk. |
| YouTube Shorts | 3 | 12pm, 5pm | Keyword-rich titles; repurpose TikToks. |
| X | 7 | 9am, 1pm, 6pm | 1/day. Thread the before/after + build-in-public weekly. |
| Reddit | 2 | 8–10am weekdays | Value-first, disclose you're the maker, reply in comments. |
| Email | 1 | Tue/Thu 10am | Lifecycle: welcome → activate → first-reply → Pro → win-back. |
| Blog | 1 | any | SEO, 900–1200 words, internal link to download. |
| ASO | per release | — | Refresh title/subtitle/keywords/screenshots each app update. |

## Monthly theme rotation (keeps angles from repeating)

- **Week 1 — Pain & relief:** reply-paralysis, overthinking, left-on-read (dating/anxious).
- **Week 2 — Speed & polish:** clear the backlog, work/Slack/email tone (pros).
- **Week 3 — Language & reach:** translator, ESL confidence, AR lens (learners/esl).
- **Week 4 — Trust & build-in-public:** on-device privacy vs rivals, founder notes.

## Launch sprints (run alongside the daily cadence)
- **Product Hunt** launch day → coordinate X thread + Reddit r/sideproject + email blast.
- **App Store feature push** → new screenshots + ASO refresh + 3 demo Shorts in 48h.
- **Reaction to platform moments** (new iOS, AI news) → 1 timely X post + 1 Short.

## What to measure (add to a weekly review)
- Installs by source (App Store / Play referrer, gorevert.com UTM links).
- Per-channel: views → profile taps → link clicks.
- Activation: % who enable the keyboard, % who send a first AI reply.
- Free → Pro conversion.
Feed the winners back into `brand.json` (promote the angles/hooks that convert).
